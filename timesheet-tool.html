Carer Timesheets

Review the shifts read from the logbook, edit if needed, download the PNG to send, and save the period to the shared Google Sheet. Nothing leaves your browser except the Google Sheet save.

How hours are counted: total hours = (Date out + Time out) − (Date in + Time in) — i.e. every hour the carer is present, including multi-day shifts. Day of week is filled in from the date automatically.

1. Pay period & rates

Period start/end power the sum-check (e.g. 1 Jun 07:00 → 16 Jun 07:00 = 360h). For double-rate holidays, tick “2×” on the row.

2. Load shifts from logbook

Each pay period: in Cowork, attach the new logbook photo and paste the prompt below. Copy the rows it returns into the box here and click Load.

Copy-paste prompt for Cowork
Read this carer logbook photo into shift rows for my timesheet tool. Output ONLY lines, one shift per line, no other text, in this exact format: carer, date-in (YYYY-MM-DD), time-in (HH:MM, 24h), date-out (YYYY-MM-DD), time-out (HH:MM, 24h) Rules: - carer is "Da" (Anida) or "Nun" (Anun), exactly as written in the Nurse column. - Use the logbook's Date in for date-in and Date out for date-out (shifts can span several days). - Convert am/pm to 24-hour time (7am = 07:00, 8pm = 20:00). - Infer the year from the photo header; if only "26" is shown assume 2026. - Do not compute hours or salary — just the raw rows. - If a value is unclear, add it as a 6th field note, e.g.: Da, 2026-06-01, 07:00, 2026-06-02, 07:00, CHECK time-in

Format: Da, 2026-06-04, 20:00, 2026-06-06, 07:00 (carer, date in, time in, date out, time out — optional 6th field = annotation). You can also add/edit rows by hand below.
